FAQ

The questions most people ask first.

What does it cost?
There's no fixed price list — every system is scoped to your operation, its complexity and your goals. Most engagements combine a one-time build with optional ongoing operation. We agree the exact scope and investment together on the strategy call, with no obligation.
What if I'm not technical?
That's the point. You don't need to be — I build and run the system, you just use it. I walk you through everything at handover, and the monthly partnership means you never have to maintain it yourself.
How much of my time does it take?
Very little. We need 1–2 conversations to scope it (about 2 hours total) and a short handover session. I handle the build, and after handover the system runs in the background.
What if it doesn't work the way we planned?
I fix it. I don't work on a "closed invoice" basis — I keep refining the system until it genuinely serves your business. A working system is the deliverable, which is exactly why we start with a no-pressure call to make sure we're a fit.
How long does it take to build?
Most systems are live within 1–4 weeks, depending on scope. You keep running your business while I build — and we agree the timeline up front.
How is this different from an AI course or agency?
A course teaches you to build it yourself. An agency rents you their stack. I build a system into your own accounts, hand it over so it stays yours, and keep operating it with you. You get the outcome without becoming a tech team.
