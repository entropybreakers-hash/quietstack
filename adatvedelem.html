2. How the website works

quietstack.entropybreakers.com is a static informational website. The website does not set cookies and does not use advertising trackers. Simply browsing the website does not require you to provide any personal data; you only provide data if you fill in the contact form (see section 4). Aggregate traffic measurement is described in section 3.

3. Traffic measurement (Cloudflare Web Analytics)

The website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to measure traffic in aggregate (for example, the number of page views). This solution does not set cookies, does not create a unique identifier, and does not track visitors across pages or websites. The service does not store IP addresses, and no individual visitor can be identified from the measurement. Because only anonymous, aggregated statistics are produced, a cookie banner is not required. The service is provided by Cloudflare, Inc.; information about its data processing is available in the Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

4. Contact form

A contact form can be filled in on the website. The data you provide here (name, email address, phone number, type of business, and any free text you enter) is processed solely to respond to your enquiry and to arrange a consultation. The legal basis for processing is your consent, which you give by ticking the checkbox before submitting the form. The submitted form data is forwarded to the provider's email account by the Make automation service. The data is retained for as long as the contact related to your enquiry requires, after which it is deleted.

5. Contact by email

If you write to us by email, we process the data you provide (name, email address, content of the message) in order to respond to your enquiry and maintain contact. The legal basis for processing is your consent and our legitimate interest in handling the enquiry. The data is retained for as long as the contact requires, after which it is deleted.

6. Invoicing

In the event of an order, we issue an invoice for the service fee. The data required for invoicing (name or company name, address, tax number, description of the service) is processed to fulfil obligations under accounting and tax law, and retained for the period prescribed by law. We use the sevDesk invoicing software (sevDesk GmbH) to issue and store invoices.

7. Hosting and technical logs

The website is hosted by GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). In the course of serving the website, the hosting provider may keep technical logs (for example, IP address, browser type), which it processes for operational and security purposes. GitHub's own privacy policy applies to this.

8. Processors

  • GitHub, Inc.: hosting (serving the website)
  • Cloudflare, Inc.: traffic measurement (Web Analytics)
  • Make.com: receiving and forwarding the submitted contact-form data
  • sevDesk GmbH: invoicing software (issuing and storing invoices)
