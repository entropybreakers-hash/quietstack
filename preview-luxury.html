Our philosophy

Growth should create leverage,
not operational chaos.

Most businesses scale revenue faster than they scale systems. The result is hidden friction, inconsistent delivery, and a business that depends on the founder to keep everything moving.

QuietStack exists to design operational infrastructure that scales calmly: quiet in the background, structured by default, built to remove noise rather than add more tools.

About

Build a business that runs without you.

I quietly took myself out of my own business. Now I help other founders do the same.

For seven years I taught English to Hungarian professionals through Entropy Breakers. I was the curriculum, the scheduling, the customer service, the marketing. Running on willpower, not systems.

So I started building. Platforms, AI-assisted feedback loops, automated onboarding and follow-up. Quietly, the business stopped needing me everywhere. The work was never about teaching language. It was about operations.

That's the work I do now, through QuietStack. AI-assisted operational systems for service businesses, so they generate more qualified client opportunities and reclaim time.

If you're building something and you're already tired, you're in the right place.
