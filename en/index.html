FAQ

Frequently asked questions

The things most people ask before we get started.

What if I'm not technical?
That's exactly the point. You don't want to be a tech expert, you want to be a coach. I build the system, you just use it. In the training session I show you what every button does. If you have any question, you write to me and I answer.
Which tools do you use?
ChatGPT and Claude, Make.com (automation), Calendly, Notion or Google Sheets, Stripe, an invoicing tool, and whatever else suits your profession. We build on your existing systems, we don't replace everything.
How much of my time does this take?
During the build, almost none — I do that part. We need 1–2 longer conversations for the design (about 2 hours total) and a 1–3 hour training session. I handle the rest. After handover I run and optimise the system for you as part of the monthly fee, so you essentially have nothing to do with it: you just use it.
What if something doesn't work well?
Quality guarantee: I don't work with refunds, I work by refining and fixing the system until it genuinely serves you well. If something doesn't work the way we agreed, I fix it. My goal is a working system, not a closed invoice. That's exactly why we start with a free consultation: so we're both sure this is right for you.
What kind of invoice do I get?
You receive a proper business invoice, which you can expense as a business cost. We'll go through the exact invoicing details on the consultation.
Do we work online or in person?
I work entirely online — we design and set everything up over video call, so wherever you are, we can work together.
What's the difference between you and an "AI academy" type course?
An AI academy teaches. You learn from them, and you build the system yourself. If you have the time and the appetite to build it yourself, that's a fine path too. I build it for you. Meanwhile you coach. In 2–4 weeks you have a working system without having had to become a tech expert.
